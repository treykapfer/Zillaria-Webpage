Welcome to Zillaria

Zillaria is a massive world ravaged by giant beasts, raging super-storms, and turbulent volcanic activity. Many of the diverse inhabitants of this world are entrenched in massive citadels to defend themselves from giant monster attacks, while others harness the power of the world titans and their spawn to build vast, sprawling empires.

Central to all life on Zillaria is the pantheon of god-like world titans. Massive, kaiju-like beasts who shape the plane to their will, and enforce an eternal balance from the plane's core. If that balance is ever threatened, the world titans will be emerge and wreak havoc among the surface dwellers.

This world is a system-neutral setting for table-top roleplaying, currently being built out by a team of three experienced world-builders. Feel free to explore the pages and learn more about Zillaria.

Behold the Gods

Every aspect of life on Zillaria is based around the presence, worship, and/or fear of the mighty world titans. A vast pantheon of minor, major, and god titans rule over this hostile world, each shaping the geography, culture, and people of the regions they inhabit. Explore the Stomping Grounds

Massive, uninhabitable deserts, raging volcanoes, jagged, dragon infested peaks, and infinite, uncrossable oceans - this perpetually overcast plane is dangerous to any creature smaller than a tarrasque, but many have still managed to gain a foothold in this hostile world. Forge Your Character

Long ago, a great cataclysm unleashed a tempest of arcane radiation that turned many of the human, elven, and promethean inhabitants of Zillaria into humanoid reflections of the world titans. These creatures, called "mutants" by some extremist humans, make up the many races and peoples of Zillaria. Because of the unstable, wild magic that pulses across the plane, any race in your imagination likely exists somewhere on Zillaria. From dragonborn, to drow, to loxodon, or merfolk, your character can be any creature you can imagine. Check out the page on peoples an cultures for already established races within Zillaria, or contact the author with a character/concept, and we can make it part of this vast world.

Unlock the Past

How did the dragons conquer Anghor, and bring a world titan to its knees? Many have chronicled the great expansion of the Darguul Empire, and how the dragon's seat of Malda rose to it's current height. What was this world like before the cataclysm? Well, I'm afraid the humans never kept physical records of their histories. Only the legends passed down from eons ago, by those who survived the great cataclysm, can tell us. Sift through the tales of Zillaria on the histories and legends page to find the answers you seek.
