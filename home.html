Welcome to Zillaria, the Land of Titans!

Zillaria is a massive world ravaged by giant beasts, raging super-storms, and turbulent volcanic activity. Many of the diverse inhabitants of this world are entrenched in massive citadels to defend themselves from giant monster attacks, while others harness the power of the world titans and their spawn to build vast, sprawling empires.

Central to all life on Zillaria is the pantheon of god-like world titans. Massive, kaiju-like beasts who shape the plane to their will, and enforce an eternal balance from the plane's core. If that balance is ever threatened, the world titans will be emerge and wreak havoc among the surface dwellers.
